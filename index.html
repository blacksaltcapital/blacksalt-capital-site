Strategy

BlackSalt invests primarily in public equities and fixed income through a concentrated portfolio of durable assets purchased at attractive valuations.

Capital preservation comes before opportunity. Position sizes reflect conviction, activity reflects opportunity, and we wait for clarity rather than manufacture it — acting only when the margin of safety is wide.

About

BlackSalt Capital L.P. is a Texas-based private investment partnership originally formed for family and friends. The general partner's capital is invested alongside the limited partners', aligning every decision with long-term outcomes rather than short-term incentives.

The partnership is intentionally small in scope and selective in membership — a structure designed to preserve the independence, flexibility, and patience that the strategy requires.
