Private Investment Partnership

Disciplined, long-term investment management for aligned capital.

BlackSalt Capital L.P. is a Texas-based family & friends fund investing primarily in public equities and fixed income through a selective, research-driven process.

Focus

Public stocks, fixed income, risk-aware capital allocation, and patient decision-making.

Strategy

The partnership pursues a concentrated, valuation-conscious approach centered on durable assets, measured underwriting, and attractive long-term risk-adjusted returns.

We prefer patience over activity and focus on preserving capital, sizing risk carefully, and acting only when opportunities meet a high standard of clarity and asymmetry.

About

BlackSalt Capital L.P. is a private investment partnership built around independent judgment, alignment of interests, and a sober analytical process.

The firm is intentionally focused in scope and designed to remain flexible, selective, and thoughtful across changing market environments.
